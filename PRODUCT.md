# PRODUCT

## Name
不可名状作品编年史

## Purpose
A complete static Lovecraftian/Cthulhu-inspired chronology website. It presents a curated sequence of weird-fiction works and fictional archive notes through a premium occult museum interface.

## Audience
Readers, collectors, students of weird fiction, and design-minded literature fans who want a moody but readable chronology.

## Register
brand/editorial

## Pages / sections
- First-viewport archive cover
- Featured manuscript card
- Works library
- Chronology index
- Mythos atlas
- Research/search desk
- Open-source project note

## Design constraints
- Dark abyssal editorial style
- Clear readable Chinese interface
- Timeline as the main information architecture
- Real image assets for texture, background, manuscript, and seals
- No gore and no cheap horror props
- Responsive desktop and mobile
- Static, deployable to GitHub Pages
