# DESIGN

## Direction
Antique occult archive meets modern digital museum. The page should feel like a literary archive recovered from a deep-sea observatory, not a generic Halloween page.

## Palette
Use OKLCH tokens. Deep black-green body, abyssal teal surfaces, aged parchment highlights, brass accents, foggy jade glows.

## Typography
Serif display for literary gravitas. System sans for readable Chinese UI. Mono only for short archival labels and dates.

## Motifs
Star charts, thin brass rules, manuscript cards, vertical timeline rail, generated seals, subtle tentacle silhouettes, worn paper textures, and restrained occult geometry.

## Interaction
Local-only search, filter chips, random archive selection, smooth anchor navigation, reduced-motion support.

## Responsive approach
Desktop preserves the concept split-screen hero. Tablet and mobile stack the story as cover, manuscript, timeline, library, atlas, search, and project note.
